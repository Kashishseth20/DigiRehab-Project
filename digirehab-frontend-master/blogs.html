Our Company

DigiRehab is a digital platform bringing different paramedical platforms such as Audiology, Speech-Language Pathology, Psychology, Physiotherapy, Occupational therapy, and Special Education under one umbrella to save your valuable time from unnecessary hassle. You can converse with our specialist counsellors from any place in the city, regardless of whether you are at home, at the workplace, on an excursion for work or in the midst of some recreation. Our specialist would provide you with proper guidance about your requirements, time and budget and suggest the best therapy and professionals, guaranteeing better clinical and budgetary results.

Vision

We trust that everybody ought to have quick and hassle-free access to a broad-ensured specialist, at whatever point and wherever required. This conviction and commitment bind us together here at DigiRehab. Furthermore, we aspire to ease the stress of parents whose children suffer from various impairment and disabilities.

Mission

We seek to put patient well-being and proper care first, bridging the gap between concerned parents and top professionals with more than ten years of experience, and furnishing families with true serenity through completely incorporated personality and body care. With an eye for the future, we are focused on developing our approach to easing India’s paramedical access.

At DigiRehab, we believe in reducing the frustration and helplessness among people/ parents of children with an impairment by providing a one-stop platform catering to all the needs, including counselling, diagnosis and management, with a team of expert certified professionals.
